Introduction/Background

This Statement of Work (SOW) defines the scope, deliverables, responsibilities, and expectations for The Hope Buss Revamped Website Project. This document serves as a formal agreement between The Hope Buss and Derby City Developers regarding the development, testing, implementation, and delivery of a redesigned public-facing website.

The Hope Buss is a nonprofit organization dedicated to community outreach and support services. The organization’s existing website does not fully meet current usability, accessibility, and engagement needs. The Revamped Website Project is intended to modernize the site, improve user experience, improve donations and volunteer engagement, and provide a scalable platform for future growth.

Derby City Developers has been engaged to design, develop, test, and deploy the new website in alignment with the approved project requirements of The Hope Buss.

Scope of Work

The scope of work for The Hope Buss Revamped Website Project includes the planning, design, development, testing, deployment, and documentation of a new public-facing website.

In scope activities include:

  • Requirements analysis and validation
  • Website architecture and UI design
  • Front-end and back-end development
  • Accessibility and usability considerations
  • Integration of third-party services (e.g., donations, contact forms)
  • System and user acceptance testing
  • Deployment

Out of scope activities include:

  • Ongoing content creation beyond initial provided materials
  • Long-term maintenance beyond project closure unless separately agreed upon

Period of Performance

The period-of-performance for this project will begin upon formal approval of this Statement of Work and will continue through project completion.

Estimated date of completion: 04/21/2026

Any changes to the period of performance will require approval through the project change control process.

Place of Performance

The majority of project work will be performed remotely and in meetings by Derby City Developers. Meetings, reviews, and demonstrations will be conducted both in-person and virtually depending on circumstances.

in-person meetings will be coordinated in advance and conducted at a mutually agreed upon location.

Work Requirements

Derby City Developers will be responsible for completing the following work activities:

Project Initiation:

  • Develop a project plan, including schedule and milestones
  • Conduct a project kickoff meeting

Requirements and Design:

  • Review and confirm functional and non-functional requirements
  • Develop website design mockups and architecture
  • Obtain stakeholder approval prior to development

Development and Testing:

  • Implement approved website design and functionality
  • Integrate approved content and third-party services
  • Conduct system testing
  • Present system to client stakeholder for review and feedback
  • Support user acceptance testing

Deployment and Closure:

  • Deploy the website to the production environment
  • Provide final documentation

Acceptance Criteria

Acceptance of project deliverables will be the responsibility of The Hope Buss Project Sponsor or designated representative.

Each project phase will require formal review before proceeding to the next phase. Deliverables will be considered acceptable when they meet the documented requirements and approval criteria.

Any issues or discrepancies will be documented and resolved prior to final acceptance. Final acceptance will occur upon successful completion of the Final Demo

Other Requirements

Security and access requirements include:

  • Controlled access to hosting environments
  • Protection of organizational data
  • Compliance with basic web security best practices

No special security clearances are required for this project.
