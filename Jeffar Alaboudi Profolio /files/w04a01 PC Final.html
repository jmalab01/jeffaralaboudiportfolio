PROJECT OVERVIEW

PROBLEM

OR ISSUE

The Hope Buss’s current website does not adequately support usability, accessibility, or engagement needs. The existing platform limits the organization’s ability to effectively communicate its mission, attract donors, and engage volunteers.

PURPOSE OF PROJECT

The purpose of this project is to design and implement a modern, user-friendly, and accessible website that strengthens The Hope Buss’s online presence and supports its mission of community outreach and support services.

BUSINESS CASE

As a nonprofit organization, The Hope Buss relies heavily on public awareness, donations, and volunteer participation. A redesigned website will improve user experience, increase credibility, and provide clear pathways for donations and volunteer involvement, ultimately supporting organizational sustainability and growth.

GOALS / METRICS

The primary goal of this project is the successful launch of a redesigned public-facing website. Project success will be measured using the following metrics: website usability feedback, accessibility compliance, increased online engagement, and improved donation and volunteer inquiry activity.

EXPECTED DELIVERABLES

·      Fully redesigned and functional public website

·      Donation, Volunteer, and Form plug-in integrations

·      Documentation for site usage and basic administration

·      Documentation outlining project processes and iterations

STATEMENT OF WORK

 

 

PROJECT SCOPE

WITHIN SCOPE

Derby City Developers will plan, design, develop, test, and deploy a revamped website for The Hope Buss. This includes requirements validation, UI/UX design, front-end and back-end development, accessibility considerations, system testing, and deployment.

OUTSIDE OF SCOPE

Ongoing content creation beyond initial materials provided by The Hope Buss and long-term website maintenance after project closure are excluded unless addressed through a separate agreement.
