6. Assume that some computers used a 22-bit word to store numbers. What is the decimal range for this word size for:

 

(a) unsigned numbers:           ______0 to 4,194,303________

 

(b) signed numbers:               _______-2,097,152 to 2,097,151________

 

What is the number of unique patterns 22 bits can store? ___4,194,304____

 

 

For unsigned, it would be 2^22 – 1 to 0 or 0 to 4,194,303.

For signed, we have to remove one from the power, and it becomes -2^21 to 2^21 - 1 for the range.

This equates to -2,097,152 to 2,097,151

For unique patterns, it becomes 2^22 because each bit can be either 0 or 1, so 4,194,304
